About

I'm Marie and welcome to my food blog. Here I am sharing with you easy-to-make recipes that I use to cook on a daily basis. You might also want to check out the bakery section for some sweet moments!

I enjoy working out regularly as well as being in nature, and as such my recipes tend to contain a healthy portion of carbohydrates and be environmentally sound (i.e. plant-based).
